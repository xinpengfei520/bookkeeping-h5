Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We highly value your privacy and only collect essential user information:

  • Account Information: Including your Apple ID login information (used only for authentication)
  • Financial Data: Including bills, budgets, assets, and other information you enter while using the app
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage data for service improvement

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
  • Enabling cross-device data synchronization
  • Delivering personalized user experiences
  • Sending service-related notifications

We commit to not using your information for any purposes beyond those stated above.

3. Information Protection

To protect your information security, we implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Data transmission encryption
  • Secure storage mechanisms
  • Access control
  • Regular security assessments

We continuously update and strengthen these protective measures to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of your information.

4. Information Sharing

We commit to:

  • Never selling your personal information to any third parties
  • Not sharing your information with unrelated third parties
  • Only sharing necessary information with your explicit authorization

We may disclose your information when required by law, but we will ensure such disclosure is limited to the necessary extent.
